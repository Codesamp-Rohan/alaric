📜 Rules & Important Messages – pearMaze

🚀 Welcome to pearMaze!

pearMaze is a decentralized platform that allows seamless sharing and discovery of peer-to-peer applications. To ensure a smooth experience for all users, please follow these guidelines:

📌 General Rules:

1. Respect Privacy & Security – Do not share sensitive information publicly. Ensure that the apps you use or share are safe.

2. No Malicious Content – Avoid adding or running apps that contain harmful code, malware, or unethical scripts.

3. Fair Usage – Do not spam or overload the network with excessive data requests or unnecessary apps.

4. P2P Ethics – Since pearMaze relies on P2P networks, be mindful of what you share and respect others' bandwidth and resources.

5. Open Source & Transparency – If you share an app, consider providing a clear description and repository link for transparency.

⚠️ Important Notes:

1. Data Storage – pearMaze caches app data for faster loading. If you face any issues, try clearing the cache or restarting the app.
